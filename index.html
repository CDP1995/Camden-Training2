Elevators

-What to do & look for?
As soon as we find out an elevator is malfunctioning, we have to make sure nobody else is using it for their safety. To do that we will have to:

  • Grab some sign from the bottom drawer that said elevator is out of service.
  • Place one sign outside and one inside of the elevator for residents to see as soon as they walk in.
  • Grab some cones to place in front of the door if it is open on one of the floors, and one more cone will be placed in front of the door at the first floor.
  • Turn off the light inside of the cabin. It is not recommended to always place the elevator on independent mode. If the elevator is malfunctioning but it is still in service we will have to open the box with the loading dock key to place it on independent mode.

-Who to call when it’s down?
When the elevators are down we have to call Kone (phone number located in our excel sheet). You just report which elevator is down, what happened to it. Once you are done the agent will give you a reference number that you will send to our manager/Waza in the WhatsApp group chat.
